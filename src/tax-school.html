The 8-Week Curriculum Breakdown

An incremental progression taking you completely from baseline foundational tax rules to advanced structural execution metrics.

Week 01

Foundations of the Tax System & Filing Requirements

Overview of the internal revenue system layout, understanding gross income benchmarks, determining filing status accurately, and standard dependent evaluation rules.

Week 02

Sourcing Income & Adjustments to Gross Income

Analyzing type structures for income generation (W-2 parameters, investment schedules, self-employed pathways) and executing Above-the-Line adjustment items correctly.

Week 03

Itemized Deductions vs. Standard Deductions

Deep dive into Schedule A dynamics including medical thresholds, mortgage tracking criteria, state and local constraints (SALT), and charitable allocation validation rules.

Week 04

Self-Employment Income & Schedule C Optimizations

Mastering independent contractor reporting metrics, tracking business expense validation loops, understanding home office parameters, and estimating self-employment liabilities correctly.

Week 05

Tax Credits & Capital Asset Allocations

Navigating refundable versus non-refundable credit matrices alongside baseline introductions to capital gains and losses scheduling protocols.

Week 06

Corporate Structures & Entity-Level Introduction

Analyzing key structural design shifts between standard LLC frameworks, complex S-Corporation tax rules, and operational partnership filings.

Week 07

Software Execution Loops & Practical Live Case Studies

Hands-on validation loops directly inside cloud-based professional software stacks translating realistic source data packets cleanly into complete, error-free filings.

Week 08

Practice Management, Client Interviews & Advisory Frameworks

Developing structural interview workflows, legal intake boundaries, document security requirements, and long-term career placement or independent firm scaling setups.
